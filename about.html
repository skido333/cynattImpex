How We Started

Our Story

Cynatt Impex Supermarket was established in 2016 in Kumasi, Ghana, with a simple but powerful goal: to make quality everyday products accessible and affordable to every household. What began as a modest retail shop has grown into a well-stocked supermarket trusted by thousands of customers.

Over the years, we have expanded our product range, improved our facilities, and built a team of dedicated staff who share our passion for excellent service. Today, Cynatt Impex stands as a go-to destination for groceries, household items, personal care products, and much more.

Our journey has been driven by the loyalty of our customers and our unwavering commitment to delivering value. As we grow, we remain rooted in the community and committed to serving our customers with the same warmth and dedication that has defined us from the beginning.
